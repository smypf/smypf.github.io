Coaching Mag has a dumbbell routine I've been using but their website has too much going on. To make it easier for me to remember what the routine is I've copied it over here.

Routine 1 - Chest & Back

Exercise Reps Sets Tempo
Floor Press
Hammer Bent-Over Row
Rest - 60 seconds
Dumbbell Push Up
Reverse Flye
Rest - 60 seconds
Wide Dumbbell Push Up
Renegade Row

Exercise Reps Sets Tempo

Rest - 60 seconds
